Delete Account Request

Welcome to the account deletion instructions page for Shopygram. If you would like to delete your account, please follow the steps below:

  • Log in to your Shopygram account.
  • Navigate to the account settings or profile section.
  • Look for the option to delete or deactivate your account.
  • Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm your request.
  • For additional assistance, please contact our customer support team at support@lofigramofficial@gmail.com.
